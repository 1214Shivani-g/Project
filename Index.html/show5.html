Sanam Teri Kasam

Re-releasing on 7th Feb, this heartfelt drama follows Inder, a rugged lone wolf stuck in an unfulfilling relationship, and Saru, a shy librarian who has faced countless rejections.
Fate brings them together when Saraswati is thrown out of her home, and Inder becomes her unwavering support. As their bond deepens, love blossoms, but destiny has other plans,
testing their love in unexpected ways.
